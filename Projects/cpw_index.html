Advantages of WPCs

What do you get when you install WPCs

There are many advantages of installing WPCs and some of them are .....

WPCs do not fade
WPCs are highly resistant to rot, termite, decay, and Marine Borer attack, though they do absorb water into the wood fibers embedded within the material.
WPCs have good workability and shaped using advanced technical machinery.
WPCs are considered a sustainable material because they are made of using recycled plastics and the waste products of the wood industry.
WPC materials continue to last for a longer period of time.
WPCs can be recycled easily, much like concrete.)
Ability of the WPCs to be moulded to meet almost any desired shape.
Another Major advantage of these materials is their lack of need for paint.
WPCs are manufactured in a variety of colours.
WPCs can be varnished and polished as per client's requirements.
WPCS can combat against any extreme weather conditions. For example, very high or low temperature does not relatively affect WPC products.
