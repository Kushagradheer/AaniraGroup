About Us

Aanira Group is a conglomerate of companies founded 8 months ago by CEO Nitin Patel. The group has five companies under its umbrella, including V.R. Buildcone and Creativeplastwood

Another substantial and remarkable company under the Aanira Group is V. R. Buildcone, which specializes in the construction industry. One of its latest projects is a residential construction project named "Aanira One", which is the company's 5th venture to be successfully completed. V.R. Buildcone has established a reputation for delivering high-quality construction projects and has become a trusted name in the industry. The company is committed to using innovative technologies and materials to create buildings that are both sustainable and aesthetically pleasing. With its focus on quality and customer satisfaction, V.R. Buildcone is poised to continue its growth and success in the construction industry.

Aanira Group is a Construction and WPC (wood plastic composite) industry company founded in 2022. The company is headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, and has established a reputation for delivering high-quality construction projects and innovative WPC products. Aanira Group has established itself as a reputable company in its industry, and its continued success is a testament to its commitment to delivering high-quality products and services.

Under the leadership of Nitinkumar Patel (CEO), Aanira Group has grown significantly over the years, expanding its operations to several regions in India. With an in-house marketing, accounting and technical team, the company has a staff of experienced, dynamic and driven professionals who are dedicated to delivering projects that meet and exceed customer expectations. Aanira Group has completed several construction projects across various sectors, including residential, commercial, and industrial.

Creativepolyplast is another company under the Aanira Group umbrella that specializes in manufacturing and supplying high-quality plastic products. The company has a wide range of products, including packaging materials, household products, and industrial supplies. The team at Creativepolyplast is committed to providing innovative solutions that meet the needs of its clients while ensuring the highest standards of quality.
